## Description (4000)
```
Chau7 Remote is the companion app for Chau7 on macOS. It lets you keep tabs on the AI coding agents running in your Mac's terminal — Claude, Codex, and shell commands — and step in the moment one needs a decision, without being at your desk.

WHAT YOU CAN DO
• Approve or deny commands an agent wants to run, right from your phone.
• Answer interactive prompts (Yes / No / numbered choices) or type a custom reply.
• Watch live terminal output for the tab you're focused on.
• Get time-sensitive notifications — including on the lock screen — the instant an approval or prompt appears.
• Follow long-running work at a glance with Live Activities and the Dynamic Island.

HOW IT WORKS
Chau7 Remote pairs with Chau7 on your Mac by scanning a QR code. It then connects through an encrypted relay — no port forwarding, no VPN, nothing exposed on your network. Every message between your phone and your Mac is end-to-end encrypted; the relay only passes sealed traffic it cannot read.

PRIVACY BY DESIGN
• Your terminal content travels only between your own devices — it is never collected by us.
• Optional diagnostics stay on your device until you choose to export them.
• No tracking, no ads, no third-party analytics.

REQUIREMENTS
• A Mac running Chau7 with Remote enabled.
• iOS 18 or later.

Chau7 Remote is a companion to the Chau7 desktop app and needs a paired Mac to be useful.
```
