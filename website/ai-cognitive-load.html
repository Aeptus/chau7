What does lower cognitive load mean in a terminal?

Cognitive load is the mental effort you spend figuring out what is happening instead of doing your actual work.

1

Traditional terminals are blind

Most terminals show you tabs with process names. That is all. You switch to each tab, read the output, figure out what is running, remember which tab is which, and repeat. Every context switch costs you attention.

2

AI agents multiply the problem

Running multiple AI agents means multiple tabs doing things you cannot see. Which agent is in which tab? Is it still running? Did it finish? Did it fail? Other terminals cannot answer any of these questions. You have to look.

3

Chau7 does the looking for you

Chau7 detects AI agents automatically, brands each tab with the agent's color and logo, shows live status, and auto-renames tabs with meaningful titles. Your peripheral vision does the work your focused attention used to do.
